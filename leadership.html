Our Leadership

Pastor David & Bonnie Beecham Lead Pastor

Pastor David and Bonnie assumed the Lead Pastor role of FPC in December of 2009. He has been the Lead Pastor at growing and vibrant church in Bloomington, IN for several years and we know that under his direction, God will lead FPC into a great and mighty future. Pastor David and Bonnie, with their gentle, lighthearted manner, have already gained the love and respect of our congregation and we know that when you come to know them, you will love them as well. We look forward to everything that God has in store for His church through our new Pastor!

Bishop Bobby & Diane Beecham

Bishop Beecham along with his wife, Diane, served as Pastor at FPC for 37 years. Throughout this time, he has gained the trust of our community and the love and respect of our church family. In some cases, Bishop Beecham has baptized three generations of family members and he has become a part of the fabric of this community. His wise guidance and loving support will continue to carry FPC through changing times.

Benji Roach Associate Pastor

Benji has served at FPC since 1999 in various roles. He is currently acting as an Associate Pastor. Benji, along with his wife, April and three children, have become an important part of our church and they work tirelessly to improve FPC. We love and appreciate Benji and April and look forward to what God will continue to do through their ministry.

Nathan Clark Worship & Music Ministries

Nathan has been acting music director at FPC since October 1999. Together with his wife, Sherry and three sons, Nathan is involved in promoting worship through music and other means within the church. Nathan has been involved in music ministry leadership for more than 20 years and is an active songwriter and studio musician.

Nick Wilson Associate Pastor

Nick Wilson has served on the trustee board as well as his current role as Associate Pastor.
